Our Story

Born from a golden era

Step inside and you're transported. Vintage radios, wall-to-wall memories, and a menu that celebrates bold flavors — 80's Café is unlike anywhere else in Cairo.

Two floors of atmosphere, a superfood menu that's genuinely delicious, and the kind of staff who make you feel at home from the first hello. We're open all day, every day.
